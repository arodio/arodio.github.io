Publications

  • 2024.10.19
    FedStale: Leveraging Stale Updates in Federated Learning
    European Conference on Artificial Intelligence (ECAI)
    This paper introduces FedStale, a novel federated learning algorithm that updates the global model in each round through a convex combination of “fresh” updates from participating clients and “stale” updates from non-participating ones.
  • 2024.07.03
    Client heterogeneity in federated learning systems
    Université Côte d'Azur
    This thesis focuses on providing a theoretical understanding of federated learning systems under heterogeneous client participation, analyzing the impact of this heterogeneity on the convergence of federated learning algorithms, and proposing practical solutions for a more efficient system and resource usage.
  • 2023.08.29
    Federated Learning Under Heterogeneous and Correlated Client Availability
    IEEE INFOCOM 2023, IEEE/ACM Transactions on Networking
    This paper presents the first convergence analysis for a federated learning algorithm under heterogeneous and correlated client availability. Our analysis highlights how correlation adversely affects the algorithm’s convergence rate and how the aggregation strategy can alleviate this effect.
